ABOUT ME

Madeleine Min

I'm a second year undergraduate at the University of Chicago, majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Media Arts and Design. I currently work at the Computer Science Instructional Laboratory (CSIL), providing on-campus (now virtual) technical support and working on the development of our project wiki. PROJECTS

What I've Worked On


VR Wind Haptic Vive Extension

Winter 2020

This attachment to a Vive headset uses an Arduino Uno and angled fans on each side of the head to produce the sensation of 'wind' corresponding to the speed and direction of travel within a Unity racing game.

SoundSNEK

Winter 2020

This is an audio-controlled classic snake game. Pitch of the input controls turning, while volume controls game speed.

See a demo here

NYT Explore

Summer 2019

NYT Explore is a stylized, location-based mapping of all Times journalism regarding cultural elements like food/dining, music festivals, art galleries. Built with Node.js, React, and Go. I worked on this project with a team of interns on Friday's during my internship at the New York Times.

Check out the Github here.
